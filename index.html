This Study Planner & Time Management Tool is designed to help students of all levels: middle school, high school, and college, organize their homework, assignments, and study sessions efficiently. By using this tool to Visualize tasks, estimated completion times, and breaks, students can manage their workload without feeling overwhelmed.

The tool uses the Most Important Task (MIT) Method to prioritize the most challenging assignments first, helping students make the most of their attention span. It also incorporates strategic 10-minute breaks, proven to improve memory retention and reduce cognitive fatigue, allowing students to study smarter, not longer.

With this planner, a student can:

  • Track assignments and homework by subject and difficulty level
  • Visualize a multi-day schedule to balance work and rest
  • Boost productivity with scientifically-backed breaks
  • Manage time effectively and reduce last-minute stress

Whether you want to plan your entire week of homework or just focus on a single study session, this tool helps you take control of your time and improve your academic performance.

Start organizing your study schedule today and make every minute count!

1. Is this study planner free?

Yes! This Study Planner is 100% free and designed for students at levels: middle school, high school, and college. You can organize your assignments, visualize your schedule, and track your workload without paying a dime. No login required either!

2. How do I use this planner?

To use this planner, simply click on the "Start Planning!" button. Then using the controls section, add the required assignments, adjust dropdown values, and click "Calculate". Alternatively, the "Preset Study Plans" provide a faster alternative to creating a schedule.

3. Can using this tool actually help me improve my grades?

This planner helps you stay organized and manage your study time more effectively. By prioritizing important tasks, planning breaks strategically, and visualizing your workload, it can reduce stress and make it easier to complete assignments efficiently, which supports better learning habits, although there is no guarantee for specific grades .

4. Will this planner work even if I have extracurricular activities or a busy schedule?

Yes! The planner is flexible and designed to fit your real-life schedule. You can add as many assignments as needed, delete specific assignments and even stop your study session momentarily by clicking the "Pause Timer" button in case of last minute changes.

5. What if I misjudge how long an assignment will take?

If you finish an assignment earlier than expected, the "Skip To Next Assignment" button allows you to move on to the next task immediately. Additionally, The planner's built-in pop-up after each assignment allows to extend study times.

6. Can this planner help me manage my workload without burning out?

Yes! This tool doesn’t just track assignments. It helps you plan breaks strategically, prioritize challenging tasks first, and spread work evenly across days. By visualizing your workload, you can maintain productivity without feeling overwhelmed.
